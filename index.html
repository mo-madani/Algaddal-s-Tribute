Mohammed Taha Algaddal

  • Born: 12 December 1951, Heliewa Village, Jazeera State, Sudan
  • Died: 5 July 2021 (aged 70) Doha, Qatar
  • Education: Algaddal started his university education at University of Khartoum at School of Medicine, then abandoned his studies to devote himself to writing poetry.
  • Most famous poems: Tawaqi Alkhouf (Caps of Fear), Amouna, Heliewa
